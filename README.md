# Graphic Marvel Website

## Creator

Paniah Mahakwa

## Description
This project is a professional website design for Graphic Marvel, a freelance graphic design business owned by Joachim Chigiya. The purpose of the site is to clearly communicate the services offered, showcase completed design projects, and provide an easy way for potential clients to request quotes.

The redesigned website will feature a clean, modern layout that follows standard web conventions and prioritizes usability. Visitors should be able to quickly understand what the business offers, browse visual examples of past work, and contact the designer without confusion. The design emphasizes clarity, professionalism, and trust-building through strong visuals and organized content.

## Target Audience

_**Primary:** 
Graphic Marvel: Joachim Chigiya — the business owner and designer.
He needs a website that effectively presents his services, highlights his portfolio, and helps potential clients reach out easily. The site must reflect his design expertise and support the growth of his freelance business.

_**Secondary:** 
Small business owners, event organizers, and community leaders who need professional branding, signage, web design, or promotional materials.

These users value clarity, reliability, and simple navigation. They want to view examples of past work, compare service options, and request quotes quickly without technical barriers.

## Project Goals
Create a clean, professional website that reflects the quality and versatility of Graphic Marvel’s design services.

Showcase completed projects with strong visual examples to build credibility and attract new clients.

Provide a simple, intuitive way for users to request quotes or contact the designer.

Clearly communicate available services such as branding, signage, web design, and event materials.

Ensure the site is easy to navigate for users with moderate tech comfort.

Strengthen trust by presenting a polished, organized, and visually consistent online presence.
